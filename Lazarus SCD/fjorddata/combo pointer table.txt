01 0F 02 0F 01 = 
Combine/Add to (function 01) HG Bullets (item 0F) with Handgun (item 02), and the reverse, as well.
The structure defines that Bullets/Handun can be combined with Handgun/Bullets.
*02 seems to exchange the ammunition, so that a Clip with 4 bullets will be placed inside a Handgun with 12,
and the 12 bullets will be placed outside the gun as a 12 bullet clip.
